About Blubu The Blahaj

Blubu is a blue blahaj from the land of Ikea, and he is celebrating his first birthday! He adores being kneaded by cats and rolling around in the dryer to get nice and toasty.

  • You MUST bring an ocean-themed plush

    If you would like to attend, you must bring a plush along with you that isn't another blahaj! Remember, this is Blubu The Blahaj's special day!

  • Be on time

    We are going to start almost right away, so be sure you don't run late! Time is of essence, and we just ask that you respect that!

    While we want you to have the utmost fun, we also ask that the activities don't get too chaotic. Play safe with your friends and enjoy the food and fun!
